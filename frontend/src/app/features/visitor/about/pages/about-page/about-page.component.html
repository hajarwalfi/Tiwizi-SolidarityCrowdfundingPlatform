Tiwizi is a Moroccan crowdfunding platform that connects passionate project creators with a community of donors who believe in real, lasting change.

"Tiwizi" — a Tamazight word meaning collective solidarity. It's the spirit of a community that comes together to support one another. That's exactly what we're building.
